Recipe #1

  • Time: Until it is frozen
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
strawberry1 pound
honey1/4 cup

Step 1:Hull the strawberries by using a straw to push out the stem of each strawberry.

Step 2:Cut strawberries in half and place on a parchment paper-lined baking sheet and freeze until hardened.

Step 3:Add the frozen strawberries and honey to a food processor and combine until evenly mixed.

Step 4:Transfer to a loaf pan and transfer to freezer until completely frozen.

Step 5:Enjoy!

Recipe #2

  • Time: 8 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
greek yogurt1 cup
flour1 cup

Step 1: In a large bowl, mix together yogurt and flour until it forms a smooth dough.

Step 2: Transfer the dough to a floured surface. Divide into 4 equal parts.

Step 3: Roll out each piece of dough to about 8 inches (20 cm) in diameter, using plenty of flour to prevent sticking or tearing.

Step 4: Cook the bread in a dry pan over medium-high heat for 2-4 minutes per side, until golden brown and cooked through.

Step 5: Enjoy!

Recipe #3

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
chocolate chips9 oz
large eggs4

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 325°F (170°C). Grease a round cake pan and line with parchment paper.

Step 2: Add the chocolate chips to a microwave-safe bowl and melt completely, about 1 minute.

Step 3: Separate the egg yolks from the whites. With an electric hand mixer, whisk the whites until soft peaks form.

Step 4: Add the egg yolks, one at a time, the bowl of melted chocolate and stir to combine.

Step 5: Using a rubber spatula, gently fold ⅓ of the whipped egg whites at a time into the chocolate mixture.

Step 6: Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top. Bake the cake for 30 minutes.

Step 7: Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top. Bake the cake for 30 minutes.

Step 8: Once the cake is cool to the touch, gently invert it onto a serving plate. Slice and dust with powdered sugar and/or serve with ice cream and fruit, if desire.

Step 9:Enjoy!
